Transfer Finder Example

Sample Route

While Google Maps and other map search engines give you subway routes and transfers, they don't always give the best option.

Let's say we want to take a trip from the 86th St Station (1 Train) to the Simpson St Station (2 Train) on Sunday at 8:00 am.

Google Maps Results

A search on Google Maps returns two possible routes. One in which we take the 1 Train to 96th St where we wait 12 minutes to transfer to the 2 Train, and one in which we walk 9 minutes to 96th St to get on the 2 Train.

Transfer Finder Results

Using the Transfer Finder, we find that if we take the 1 Train south two stops from 86th St, we can transfer to the 2 Train with only a 2.5 minute wait. This total time for this trip is also shorter than both of the trips Google Maps suggested.
